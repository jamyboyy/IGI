I.  Who we are 001 / 005
Head Office · Lagos
Est. 31 October 1991

Underwriting what matters, since 1991.

IGI was first incorporated on 31 October 1991 and re-registered as a public limited liability company in 2007. In 1996 we made history as the first private insurance company in Nigeria to post ₦1 billion in premium income.

That same year the federal government appointed us as lead consortium member for the Nigerian National Petroleum Corporation's insurance programme. In 2005 we expanded across Africa, acquiring controlling stakes in National Insurance Corporation (Uganda), Sonarwa (Rwanda), and Gamstar (Gambia).

Today, IGI remains a composite insurer serving banking, oil, energy, manufacturing, engineering, marine, aviation, and government sectors.
